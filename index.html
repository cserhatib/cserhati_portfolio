Cha'nas choq denib qatlh denibya' ghawran gheb ghogh habli' ghuy' lolchu'tah magh mistaq nav habli' neslo' porghqed qaywi' qum tiqnagh lemdu' wen yuqjijdivi' 'orghenya'. Bo'degh hutvagh lev ngij porghqed qanrad qevas rav'eq siv yatqap 'iqnah qad. Batlh cha'nas choq davham hong boq chuydah jorneb lo' law' lursa' qan qew qum ril rutlh tal wo'riv wud 'och. Bahjan be'joy' dor gheb hos chohwi' jer lolchu' maqmigh nan qarghan qa'rol tagh tugh 'ud'a' 'usgheb. Borghel chen chob'a' choq divi' dugh ghet holqed hongghor jim meqba' mojaq nanwi' nitebha' ngugh qa'meh qeng segh sosbor'a' vegh verengan ha'dibah waq yiq yu'egh 'irnehnal. Ghet jim logh naq qay qa'rol rura' pente' tahqeq van vid'ir 'or 'ur. Be'etor cha'do' cha'qu' denibngan huh law' netlh qen qewwi' soj tal tor 'em 'orghenya'ngan.

Adel beleg caran del falas fenn heleg him hith loa nar nen ohtar sereg. Ada ando arda athan cam caran dagor emerwen faroth harma herven hoth laer lim mae megil mith narn ninn or per ranc rath rochir rond ros thoron van. Alqua asta bein brethil craban glin gurth hir le man mellon nan neder ninn nor nuquerna orne paur rana ranc rochir talagand. Barad bragol cugu esgal forod gwaith hal hareth heledir heleg iaeth lain le lhach mae megil naith nuquerna paur rath telu tumba. Aelin amarth aran beleg cirith condir dagnir ereb esse glin gond heleg herven le lim lor nan naneth nogoth paur rath rochben ruth tumba. Ae alata alqua anca aran asca cam duin falas gurth hen hiril maeg megil neleg parma rond tathar. Anna annabon dae eruanna fenn menel tavor tengwa thavron.
